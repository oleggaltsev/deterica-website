FAQ

Questions, answered.

Everything you need to know before requesting access.

Which marketplaces does Deterica support?

Deterica is built first for Amazon and eBay, the marketplaces where listing quality and price discipline most directly decide who wins. Walmart, Etsy, and Shopify support are on the roadmap.

Will the AI write listings that break marketplace policy?

No. Deterica generates copy against each marketplace's content rules and character limits, and flags anything that risks suppression — so what you publish is both competitive and compliant.

Do I have to trust the price it recommends?

Never blindly. Every price recommendation comes with the reasoning shown — the rival prices, margin impact, and demand signals behind it. You stay in control of the final number.

Can it improve listings I've already published?

Yes. Connect your catalog and Deterica scores every live listing, then hands you a prioritized fix list so you improve the listings that move revenue first — with one-click apply and A/B comparison.

Is my account and sales data safe?

We connect through official marketplace APIs, encrypt data in transit and at rest, and never sell your data or expose it to other sellers. Your competitive edge stays yours.
